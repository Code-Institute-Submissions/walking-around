Hikes for Everyone

Our community enjoy different types of hikes, from the family trails available for the youngest to the highest pics and everything in between!
We organize half day and full day trips on the weekends. During holidays we organize long hikes and treks for different experience.

  • Nature knowledge
  • Camping
  • and more ...

Meet Up

Meetings are scheduled around a coffee once a week and are for everyone to participate. This is where we talk about ideas and organize the next events.
Then we post everything on our private group chat so all members are kept in the loop.
For specific queries a different chat channel is available.
