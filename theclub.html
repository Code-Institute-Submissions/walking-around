About Us

We are a Club that welcomes everyone, either beginners or advanced hikers with no age limit.
We are a community of Nature enthusiast and we love going out there to enjoy every bit of it.
Based in Bray county Wicklow (Ireland's Garden), We aim to Discover our beautiful country in every nook and cranny ;).
With us you can Discover, Explore, Learn, Challenge Yourself and have some fun!
If you are interested in joining and sharing our Passion, get in touch by clicking on the "Join Us!" button on the bottom-right of the screen and fill up the form ! One of us will get back to you soon.
We are very excited to meet you!
